Constraints

Interval

1 day 14 days

FPFool uses your browser history to visit websites you already visited. In doing so, FPFool uses your most recent browser history entries. The interval defines what "recent" means.

Website limit

5 20

Sometimes, there may be a lot of browser history entries even in a short period of time. This option allows you to limit the number of different websites that should get visited by this tool.

Tab limit

3 10

The tab limit is the maximum amount of tabs being open at the same time.


Connection limit factor

1 5

This setting allows you to limit the total amount of connections being made (not more than the selected number times the usual amount of connections from the user per day).
